Rehearsal Schedules

What to Know Before You Go

← Back to Main Recital Page

Before Rehearsal

  • Check the schedule—times/days may change and multiple rehearsals may be required. Attend all.
  • Be on time.
  • Drop off/pick up only at assigned times to keep traffic flowing.
  • Label all dance shoes (name or initials inside).

What to Bring

  • Only necessary dance shoes in a small bag.
  • No costumes.
  • No toys, tablets, or fidgets.

During Rehearsals

  • Drop off only—no parents allowed inside (please read the information sent home with your dancer).
  • Expect a busy parking lot; use the field or mailbox drop-off if possible (for older dancers).
  • No snacks or meals—eat before or after.
