Show Troupe

Excellence on Stage

Show Troupe is a performing group that performs in Jazz, Tap, Contemporary, Musical Theatre, Hip-Hop and Lyrical. You may see them around the community, dancing at various events and festivals.

In previous years, Show Troupe has performed at Disney Springs, Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines, and in front of the Statue of Liberty in New York City.
