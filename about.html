Our Philosophy

What Makes Us The Right Choice?

With more than 40 years of teaching experience, Misty and her staff are professionals and dedicated to helping each and every student achieve success. Our teachers are well trained, qualified to teach and required to stay abreast of developments in education and performing arts by attending conventions and dance classes for their continued education. We are members of both Dance Masters of America and Dance Educators of America. These organizations require that teachers be tested both orally and in the classroom for certifications to teach.

We believe that each student has different talents that if developed correctly, will bring out the best in every child.

Why Families Love DDS

Our studio takes a unique approach to dance education that fosters development while also focusing on a child's individual strengths.

Professional Floating Dance Floor

Our studio is equipped with a professional floating floor to protect impact on the body (long term injuries can be prevalent when dancers regularly train and dance on concrete).

Personalized Attention & Small Class Size

Class sizes are limited and we never over crowd classes. Our students will learn proper technique and get individual instruction.

Annual Recital with Performance Opportunity for All Dancers

Highly acclaimed annual recitals with professional lighting and sound that are entertaining and engaging.
