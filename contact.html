Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for classes?

Registration information is available on our Current Classes page. We recommend registering early as classes fill up quickly.

What age groups do you serve?

We offer dance classes to all skill and ability levels from age 2 through 18.

What dance genres do you teach?

We teach jazz, ballet, tap, hip hop, contemporary, lyrical, and acro.

Is Dance Dimensions a competitive studio?

No, we are a non-competitive dance studio focused on building confidence and fostering a lifelong love of dance.
