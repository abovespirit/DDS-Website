DDS Dress Code

A Cohesive & Professional Look

At Dance Dimensions, we believe that a cohesive and professional appearance fosters discipline, unity, and a true sense of team spirit within our studio. To maintain this standard and to help our dancers focus on technique and performance, we have implemented a dress code for all classes across all age groups.

This uniformity not only creates a polished and professional look during class but also helps dancers feel part of a larger team, promoting confidence and camaraderie. We appreciate your cooperation in supporting this dress code, which reflects our commitment to excellence at Dance Dimensions.

Our studio dance shop sells the brands of shoes recommended for each class as well as leotards. Proper shoes and attire are required for each class.

NEW STUDENTS: Please talk to us before purchasing dance attire or shoes to ensure you are getting the proper items. Any shoes worn for classes/inside the ballroom can NOT be worn outside.

Pre-School & Kinder Classes

All Classes

  • Lavender Logo Leotard (leotards must be purchased from the studio)
  • Tan or Pink Tights (optional for Pre and Kinder 1)
  • Hair must be pulled back in a pony or bun

Shoes

Shoes worn in the ballroom can NOT be worn outside.

  • Ballet and/or Jazz Classes: Pink Split Sole Ballet Shoes (canvas or leather)
  • Tap Classes: Tan Tap Shoes
  • Acro: Barefoot

Upper Elementary, Middle & High School Classes

All Classes

  • Black Leotard
  • Optional: Fitted shorts or a skirt
  • Hair must be pulled back in a pony or bun
  • Tan or Pink Tights (required)
  • No large or excessive amounts of jewelry

Shoes

Shoes worn in the ballroom can NOT be worn outside.

  • Ballet — Pink Split Sole Ballet Shoes
  • Jazz — Tan Jazz Shoes
  • Tap — Tan Tap Shoes
  • Hip Hop — White Soled Sneakers (must stay at studio)
  • Acro — Tan Jazz Shoes or Barefoot

Questions about the dress code or need to purchase attire? Visit our studio dance shop or get in touch.
